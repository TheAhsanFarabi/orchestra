FIFA Information
================

FIFA Full Name: Fédération Internationale de Football Association
FIFA Acronym: International Association

Establishment:
- Date: January 1, 1904
- Location: The Hague, Belgium
- Founding Members: 14 football associations

Organization Type:
- International governing body
- Sport: Association Football (Soccer)
- Membership: 212 national associations

World Cup:
- Frequency: Every 4 years
- Current Host: Qatar (2022)
- Next Host: USA (2026)

Key Tournaments:
1. FIFA World Cup - International tournament
2. UEFA Champions League - Club competition
3. FIFA Club World Cup - Club competition
4. CONCACAF Champions Cup - North American club competition

Major Achievements:
- 1930: First international game in modern times (Uruguay vs Argentina)
- 1990: Won World Cup (first time)
- 2022: Qatar hosted World Cup

FIFA World Player of the Year:
- Awarded since 1997
- Current holder: Lionel Messi (as of recent awards)
